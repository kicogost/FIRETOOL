# r/SpainFIRE — what the community actually wants (research)

Source: 259 unique top/hot posts from r/SpainFIRE (2018–2026), pulled via Reddit's
API and analyzed locally (raw data kept off-repo). This is the **primary driver**
for the product roadmap and the marketing approach. Evidence = real post titles
with [upvotes / comments].

## The 8 themes (by intensity)

### 1. Taxes are the #1 emotional driver — and a credibility test
The most-engaged posts are about fiscalidad. The community feels punished for saving.
- "Do you believe income tax in Spain approaches confiscatory levels?" [89↑ 417c]
- "El sistema no incentiva el ahorro 😡 — en EEUU hay 401k, en UK ISA, aquí 1.500€ de plan de pensiones" [276↑ 153c]
- "Los fondos indexados del SP500 pagan más impuestos que los ETFs"; "Cómo declarar el fondo monetario de Revolut en la renta"; "N26 y Revolut NO reportan a Hacienda"
- **Product:** show **net (post-IRPF)** figures, model the **traspaso** fund advantage, use a Spain-adjusted (lower) SWR. Tax-awareness = instant credibility vs US/UK apps.

### 2. They DON'T trust their broker's app — even to show the right balance
MyInvestor is the de-facto FIRE broker, but its app reliability is a loud, recurring complaint.
- "NO uses MyInvestor — mi balance aparecía inflado 30k€ o en 0" [212↑ 234c]
- "¿Qué leches pasa con la App de MyInvestor?" · "MyInvestor se cae a trozos" · "[CUIDADO] Error grave: compra a precio inventado, -10%"
- **Product/marketing hook:** an independent tracker **you control** — "tu patrimonio, sin depender de la app de tu broker." This is our single strongest angle.

### 3. Explicit demand for OUR exact product (no bank connection)
- **"¿Buena app de finanzas personales sin tener que conectar bancos?"** [32c] — literally asking for us.
- "¿Soy el único al que le da respeto volcar los ahorros en una plataforma solo online?" — trust/privacy is front-of-mind.
- **Marketing:** lead with **"sin conectar tu banco · sin comisiones · tus datos son tuyos."** Manual/CSV entry is a *feature* here, not a limitation.

### 4. They already track budgets & celebrate net-worth milestones (gamification works)
- "Presupuesto de 2025 / 2024 / 2023" — annual expense breakdowns, huge engagement [243c, 134c, 110c]. They do this in spreadsheets today.
- "¡Acabo de cruzar 100k€ de NW!" · "Cruzando los 200k" · "Camino a 1M€ desde cero. Actualización #1" · "5 años invirtiendo desde 0"
- **Product:** net-worth milestones + progress updates that are **shareable** (screenshot-ready) = built-in marketing. Their budget-tracking habit validates pillar 3.

### 5. Tools/calculators get adopted when shared for feedback — the go-to-market
- "Nueva calculadora SpainFireCalc.es (FIRE España-first) — Feedback por favor" [140↑ 69c]; "Calculadora FIRE para inversores españoles – busco feedback"; "Niveles FIRE en España según tu renta" [187↑]
- "Plantilla sheets comparativa de fondos"; "explorador de fondos de MyInvestor" — people build & share utilities.
- **Marketing playbook:** ship it **España-first**, post it as *"he hecho una herramienta gratis, ¿feedback?"*, be a genuine participant. That's exactly how this sub adopts things.

### 6. Real estate vs investing + mortgage decisions — constant debate
- "¿Por qué la obsesión con los pisos?"; "¿alquilar es tirar el dinero?"; "Si vas a invertir en vivienda, mejor en fondos (ejemplo)"; "El truco de los bancos al amortizar hipoteca"; "amortizar o invertir".
- **Product:** net worth must handle property value + mortgage cleanly (we do). A **"comprar vs alquilar"** and **"amortizar vs invertir"** calculator would be catnip (learning tools).

### 7. "Is FIRE even realistic in Spain?" — realism & motivation
- "La cruda realidad del FIRE en España" [312↑ 153c]; "FIRE tiene sentido entre ingenieros de California, no entre auditores en Malasaña"; "Desmontando el mito de que FIRE es para privilegiados"; "¿Cómo os mantenéis motivados?"; "espiral de ahorro que no sé gestionar"; "¿y después de FIRE qué?"
- **Product/tone:** honest, **España-first**, no Californian fantasy. Spain-adjusted projections, acknowledge lower salaries + the public-healthcare advantage. Motivation features must feel real, not hype.

### 8. Income is seen as the real lever (but taxed hard)
- "Son los ingresos, estúpido" [204↑]; "Sueldo 2K"; "IT >75k no merece la pena por impuestos".
- **Product:** savings-rate framing with income context; show how each €/month of surplus moves the FIRE date (our core mechanic — they'll get it).

## What this means for the 3 pillars

- **Pillar 1 (Patrimonio/FIRE):** already aligned. Add Spain-tax realism (net withdrawal, ~3.5% SWR option), shareable milestone cards, property/mortgage clarity.
- **Pillar 2 (Aprender):** prioritize the topics they actually debate — traspaso (fondos vs ETF), comprar vs alquilar, amortizar vs invertir, IRPF/720 basics, broker reality (incl. honest MyInvestor pros/cons), "cuánto necesito" España-first. Tone: honest, no hype.
- **Pillar 3 (Gastos):** they already track annual budgets manually — give them automatic category analysis + rule-based cut tips (fees → neobanco, subscriptions, category spikes vs their own baseline). Framed as observation, not nagging.
